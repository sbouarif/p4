Our Mission

Hi, I’m Sameh Bouarif! I’m a college student at NJIT, majoring in Business and Information Systems. I’ve always been fascinated by how technology and business come together to solve real-world problems, which is why I chose this field. Before college, I interned at a small startup where I helped improve their e-commerce operations, and it was such a cool experience—it really got me hooked on data and process optimization. In the future, I’d love to work as a data analyst for a big company and maybe even start my own consulting business someday. Outside of school, I’m super into rock climbing, I’m fluent in French, and I’m always up for hosting game nights with my friends. (all this is made up)
