About Me

class="float-start me-3">

Hi, I’m Sameh, a second-year student at the New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT), majoring in Business and Information Systems. At just 19 years old, I’m on an exciting journey to combine the analytical power of business with the innovative world of technology.

Growing up, I was always fascinated by the way technology shapes our world. Whether it was exploring the intricacies of websites or understanding how businesses operate behind the scenes, I knew early on that I wanted to be part of this ever-evolving field. NJIT has provided me with the perfect platform to hone my skills, engage in meaningful projects, and learn from inspiring mentors and peers.

My aspirations align closely with my major—I aim to bridge the gap between business operations and cutting-edge technologies. I hope to work on implementing innovative solutions that improve efficiency, decision-making, and customer experiences for organizations. My dream is to contribute to projects that leverage big data, artificial intelligence, and system integration to create smarter, more adaptive businesses.

As I look ahead, I’m exploring career paths such as systems analyst, IT consultant, or project manager in the technology industry. These roles would allow me to utilize my understanding of both technical and business aspects to help companies navigate the digital landscape. Additionally, I’m interested in potentially working in e-commerce, fintech, or with startups, where innovation is at the forefront.

Fun Fact: I’ve already designed a mockup website for my favorite local café, combining my love for design and tech!
